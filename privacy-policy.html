Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 29, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Number10 ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Number10 (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game statistics (scores, play count, game history)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Purchase status for in-app purchases

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Game Center

If you choose to use Apple Game Center features, the following information may be shared with Apple:

  • Your Game Center display name
  • Your game scores for leaderboard rankings

This data is managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

In-App Purchases

All in-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App functionality
  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Enable Game Center leaderboard features

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Data stored locally is protected by your device's security features.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center: For leaderboards and achievements
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertisements shown are configured to be appropriate for all ages.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local game data by uninstalling the App
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Disable Game Center features in your device settings
  • Request information about what data we process

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
